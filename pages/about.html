Our Story

Advance Fleet Services is a family owned and operated company with over 40 years of experience in providing first class fleet maintenance, tractor-trailer repair, and diesel engine services. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our dedication to customer service, which sits at the core of our business values.

At Advance Fleet Services, we have established working partnerships with some of the largest transportation companies in the world, solidifying our reputation as a premier fleet service provider. Our team has meticulously developed processes, services, and quality standards to guarantee that our customers' fleets stay running strong.

We understand the importance of keeping your fleet operating efficiently, which is why we go above and beyond to ensure that every aspect of our service meets your needs. Trust Advance Fleet Services to keep your fleet on the road.
