Convenient Gate Features

  • The performance model has a 2-way spreader gate with barn door plus slide out ramps.
  • Maximize hauling equipment and material with a heavy-duty, spring-assisted, width-adjustable integrated gate and ramps on premium models for the safest gate to load and unload equipment.
  • Brandt gates are constructed with HD keeper pins and latch systems, ensuring a reliable hookup.

Durable Box Design

  • The 14' and 16' box lengths optimally fit and balance the weight of compact construction equipment.
  • Available in two box styles with Max Body™ models offering more capacity and optimized for equipment.
  • The box is built with durable 10-gauge steel with only one center seam, reducing debris buildup.

Long-Lasting Structure

  • The HSS tube frame ensures torsional stiffness when traveling with a load, extending trailer life.
  • Forward self-adjusting brakes enhance control and safety while minimizing maintenance costs.
  • Available torsion axles or spring suspension in tandem and tri-axle models deliver a smooth ride.
  • Bolt-on key components (including fenders, jacklegs, and couplers) are easy to replace and minimize downtime through more efficient maintenance.

Features

Scissor-Lift Hoist

A 3-way hydraulic power unit enables operators to lift the trailer into power up, power down, or gravity down use with the push of a button for effortless dumping or loading.

LED Lighting

Tier 1 LED lights with polycarbonate lenses, sealed connectors, and epoxy-encapsulated electronics deliver significantly more protection than any other trailers in the industry.

Premium Wiring

Braided loom wiring harness with sealed weatherpack connections meet the same rigorous standards of wires found on heavy-duty construction equipment, ensuring long-lasting protect.

Enclosed Toolbox

Versatile Hitch Styles

Bumper pull or gooseneck configurations with payloads from 10,000 lbs to 16,000 lbs deliver more versatility.

Bucket Shelf
