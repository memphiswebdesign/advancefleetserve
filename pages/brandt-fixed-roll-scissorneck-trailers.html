Ready to Roll.

Brandt’s fixed-roll scissorneck trailers give you the flexibility to get more done and the durability to keep up the pace, day after day. These trailers are compatible with a single-axle booster, so you can haul your trailer’s full-rated payload. And, our unique kicker-roll placement near the back of the trailer makes loading and unloading short payloads quick and easy. Plus, these reliable designs use long-lasting components and the toughest powder-coat finish available to maximize service life on Canada’s harshest roads.

Don’t Stop When You’re On a Roll.

The all-new S400 Fixed Roll scissorneck trailer from Brandt gives you the flexibility to get more done — no matter where the road takes you. This versatile design uses a single-axle booster to reduce your weight-per-axle, so you can breeze through seasonal road bans unimpeded. It comes equipped with a narrow flip extension, so you can add a jeep to maximize payload capacity while the narrow design minimizes your tare weight, optimizing your performance with any load. Brandt’s innovative flip extension also eliminates the swing clearance issues common among other designs, allowing you to drive on, stress-free.
