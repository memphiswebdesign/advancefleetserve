We are proud to announce our latest development, the S-TRAK system.

The S-TRAK is a customizable & customer taylored tracking system that provides:

  • Valuable service data for tracking and maintaing specialty services/preventative maintenance schedules
  • Equipment/ Asset location fields
  • Equipment/Asset unit and identifier data fields
  • Assigned asset manager contact information
  • Visually enhanced color coded warning alerts for completed, upcoming, and past due services
  • Integrated notation fields for critical unit specific information
  • User edits and system updates live and visable around the clock
  • & much more
