Easy Riders.

Brandt’s lineup of on-highway transporter trailers deliver a smooth ride and even smoother operation. We built these trailers with low tare-weight and deck-height in mind to maximize payload and save you the hassle of over-height permits. Plus, these reliable designs use long-lasting components and the toughest powder-coat finish available to maximize service life on Canada’s harshest highways.

Easy To Handle, Hard To Beat.

Brant’s Work Ready™ T300 cable steering pole trailer delivers increased uptime, low cost of operation, and higher productivity. It hauls pipe safely down highways, rural roads, and off-road areas where conditions are variable and meets legal requirements across North America, so you can complete a variety of tasks with ease. These units are designed to track with the pulling tractor, providing maneuverability that you won’t get with straight trailers. Plus, dual steer-lock pins hold the steering suspension straight for easier unloading.
