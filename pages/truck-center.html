Truck Center

At the Advance Truck Center drivers lounge you can sit and relax with a hot cup of cofee, have a snack, or charge your phone while waiting for your service to be completed.

You can even pick up some tow straps, a box of fuses, or any number of items the demanding road requires.

At the AFS Truck Center we are a one stop shop for all your trucking needs!
