Service Manager

Pay: $100,000.00 - $150,000.00 per year

Work Location: In person

Apply today →

Job Description

Advance Fleet Services is looking for a hands-on Service Team Manager to lead daily shop operations, support a team of skilled technicians, and keep our customers’ fleets moving. This role is ideal for someone who knows how a diesel shop should run, and is ready to take ownership of making it happen. You won’t be stuck in the middle managing chaos. You’ll have the support and authority to build a team, improve workflow, and create a shop environment that runs right.

What You’ll Do:

Shop Operations
  • Oversee daily workflow of the shop, ensuring jobs move efficiently from intake to completion
  • Schedule and prioritize repairs based on urgency, technician skillset, and workload
  • Monitor job progress and adjust as needed to avoid delays
  • Maintain a clean, safe, and organized shop environment
Team Leadership
  • Lead, coach, and support a team of diesel technicians
  • Set clear expectations for performance, quality, and accountability
  • Assist with hiring, onboarding, and training new team members
  • Foster a team culture built on respect, communication, and pride in work
Quality & Efficiency
  • Ensure all repairs meet company and industry standards
  • Troubleshoot complex or escalated repair issues when needed
  • Identify inefficiencies in workflow and implement improvements
  • Reduce comebacks and rework by reinforcing “fix it right the first time”
Customer & Communication
  • Serve as a point of contact for service updates when needed
  • Work closely with front office/service advisors to manage expectations and timelines
  • Ensure clear communication between technicians and leadership
Shop Performance
  • Track key metrics (turnaround time, productivity, comeback rate)
  • Manage parts usage and help control costs without cutting corners
  • Support continuous improvement of shop processes and systems

What We’re Looking For:

  • 5+ years of experience in a Service Manager role
  • Previous leadership or supervisory experience in a shop environment
  • Strong understanding of shop workflow, diagnostics, and repair processes
  • Ability to lead a team while still being hands-on when needed
  • Organized, decisive, and solutions-focused
  • Strong communication and problem-solving skills

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary (based on experience)
  • Performance-based incentives
  • Stable, consistent workload
  • A supportive ownership team that values your input
  • A shop where quality work and teamwork come first
  • Opportunity to grow with the company

Why This Role Is Different

You’ve worked in shops where everything is reactive. Where you’re expected to manage problems, but not fix them. At Advance Fleet Services, we’re building something better. This is your opportunity to take control of a shop, lead a strong team, and create a standard you’re proud of.

Apply Today — If you’re ready to lead, not just manage, we’d like to talk.

Benefits

  • 401(k)
  • Dental insurance
  • Health insurance
  • Paid time off
  • Vision insurance

Level A Diesel Technician

Pay: $30.00 - $50.00 per hour

Work Location: In person

Apply today →

Job Description

Advance Fleet Services is looking for a highly skilled Level A Diesel Technician who takes pride in diagnosing problems correctly and fixing them right the first time. This is not an entry-level role. We’re looking for someone who can work independently, handle complex diagnostics, and set the standard for quality in the shop. If you’re tired of rushed jobs, poor communication, or working with outdated equipment, this is a place where your experience is valued.

What You’ll Do:

Diagnostics & Repair
  • Diagnose and repair complex mechanical, electrical, and hydraulic issues
  • Perform in-depth troubleshooting on diesel engines, trailers, and fleet equipment
  • Handle advanced jobs where deep expertise is required
Quality Workmanship
  • Complete repairs accurately and efficiently with minimal supervision
  • Ensure all work meets company and industry standards
  • Stand behind your work, reducing comebacks and repeat repairs
Preventative Maintenance & Inspections
  • Perform thorough inspections to identify potential issues early
  • Complete preventative maintenance services with strong attention to detail
Team Support
  • Assist and mentor junior technicians when needed
  • Collaborate with the Service Manager and team to keep workflow moving
  • Communicate clearly about job status, parts needs, and potential delays
Shop Responsibility
  • Maintain tools and equipment in good working order
  • Keep your workspace clean, organized, and safe
  • Follow all safety procedures and best practices

What We’re Looking For:

  • 5+ years of hands-on diesel technician experience
  • Strong diagnostic skills (electrical, mechanical, hydraulic)
  • Ability to work independently and solve problems efficiently
  • Experience with fleet maintenance and repair
  • Own tools required (specialty tools a plus)
  • CDL and/or certifications are a plus (not required)

What We Offer

  • Competitive pay based on experience
  • Consistent hours and steady workload
  • A well-equipped shop with the right tools
  • A team that respects your time and skill
  • Leadership that supports doing the job right—not rushing it
  • Opportunity to grow and take on more responsibility

Why This Role Is Different

You’ve worked in places where:

  • You’re rushed through jobs
  • You’re fixing someone else’s mistakes
  • You don’t have the tools you need

That’s not how we operate.

At Advance Fleet Services, we believe in:

  • Fixing it right the first time
  • Supporting our technicians
  • Building a team that takes pride in their work
