Stack the Deck.

Brandt’s flip-over beavertail scissorneck trailers are optimized for back-loading with the toughest, easiest-to-use rear end on the road. The heavy-duty locking mechanism is situated between main beams to protect it from the elements, maximizing reliability. And, our unique kicker-roll placement near the back of the trailer makes loading and unloading short payloads quick and easy. Plus, these reliable designs use long-lasting components and the toughest powder-coat finish available to maximize service life on Canada’s harshest roads.

Don’t Stop When You’re On a Roll.

You’ve got a lot riding on your trailer, so we made the all-new S400 flip-over beavertail scissorneck trailer from Brandt more durable and easier to use. The extra-heavy-duty ramp locking mechanism is positioned between main beams, ensuring maximum reliability. And, the tough powder-coat finish gives the ramp superior wear-protection, allowing you to confidently load your equipment — even with ice cleats. Once your equipment is loaded, you can count on the winch guide flip-up mechanism to quickly and easily raise the ramp so you can hit the road sooner.

Payload 40 tons
Dimensions (L×W×H) 10' x 42'
