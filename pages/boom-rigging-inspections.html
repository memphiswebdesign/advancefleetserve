Inspections of boom and rigging equipment on electric trucks are crucial for ensuring the safety of workers and the efficient operation of the machinery.

Importance of inspections
  • Accident Prevention: Regular inspections will help identify and address potential hazards before they lead to accidents or injuries.
  • Regulation Compliance: Adherence to OSHA and ASME standards ensures legal compliance and avoids costly fines and penalties.
  • Extending Equipment Life: Routine maintenance and inspections can maximize the lifespan of the equipment, reducing repair costs and maximizing efficiency.
  • Worker Protection: Frequent Inspections help to ensure the safety of boom truck operators and other personnel working near the equipment is paramount.
