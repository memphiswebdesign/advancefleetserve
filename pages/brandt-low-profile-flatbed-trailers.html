Work Ready™ Brandt trailers are purpose-built with durable components and the toughest powder-coat paint finish in the industry to maximize service life. And, they are covered by best-in-class warranty and a nationwide dealer network to ensure your trailer is always ready to work when you are.

Unmatched Loading and Unloading

  • Three ramp configurations, all available with spring assists to accommodate equipment without hassle, ensuring superior versatility.
  • Dovetail with full-width ramps enables a 13º load angle, making loading and unloading more efficient.
  • Built-in knee supports on the ramps ensure the rear of the trailer is supported during loading.
  • Corrugated, cleated ramps minimize debris buildup while delivering unmatched traction.

Versatile Deck Design

  • Purpose-built for compact construction equipment with GVWR between 9,900 and 14,900 lbs.
  • UBE518 optimized for equipment up to 7,050 lbs.
  • UBE720 ideal for equipment up to 11,600 lbs.
  • Load-bearing toolbox delivers additional deck space, creating more storage in the hitch.
Easier To Operate

Efficient loading and unloading with convenient load management features that keep you productive at the worksite.

Increased Safety

Safety is critical on the jobsite, so these models are designed with key features to maximize operator protection.

Easier To Own

Purpose-built design and reliable components ensure a lifespan that matches your equipment.

Durable Structure

  • A unified-frame design integrates the frame and deck into a single, robust structure.
  • The 6”x2” and 8”x2” HSS main rails and 3”x2” HSS cross members reinforce the entire frame.
  • Dexter EZ-Lube Torflex axles with forward self adjusting electric brakes for reduced maintenance.
  • The HSS frame cases the trailer wiring, protecting it from the elements for long-lasting performance.

Features

Premium Decking

Fir decking is denser and more durable than commonly used materials to withstand harsh weather conditions, making it less prone to split under heavy use.

LED Lighting

Tier 1 LED lights with polycarbonate lenses, sealed connectors, and epoxy-encapsulated electronics deliver more protection than any other trailers in the industry.

Protected Wiring

Braided loom wiring harness with sealed weatherpack connections meet the same rigorous standards of wires found on heavy-duty construction equipment, ensuring life-long protection.

Bolt-Together Design

All major components such as axles, fenders, jacks, couplers, ramps, and ramp plates (on full-width option) are bolted together, minimizing downtime when maintenance is required.

Reliable Running Gear

Featuring two 5,200 lb or two 7,000 lb torsion axles for smooth and durable performance with a zero-maintenance suspension.

Premium Paint Protection

Premium polyurethane primer and top coat is abrasion-resistant, ensuring these trailers look better for longer while maximizing resale value.

Multiple Configurations

Brandt delivers different trim levels so you can tailor your trailer for your exact operational needs.
