Work Ready™ Brandt trailers are purpose-built with durable components and the toughest powder-coat paint finish in the industry to maximize service life. And, they are covered by best-in-class warranty and a nationwide dealer network to ensure your trailer is always ready to work when you are.

Low Profile Flatbed Trailers

Three ramp configurations, all available with spring assists to accommodate equipment without hassle, ensuring superior versatility. Dovetail with full-width ramps enables a 13º load angle, making loading and unloading more efficient. Built-in knee supports on the ramps ensure the rear of the trailer is supported during loading. Corrugated, cleated ramps minimize debris buildup while delivering unmatched traction.

Purpose-built for compact construction equipment with GVWR between 9,900 and 14,900 lbs. UBE518 optimized for equipment up to 7,050 lbs. UBE720 ideal for equipment up to 11,600 lbs. Load-bearing toolbox delivers additional deck space, creating more storage in the hitch.

A unified-frame design integrates the frame and deck into a single, robust structure. The 6”x2” and 8”x2” HSS main rails and 3”x2” HSS cross members reinforce the entire frame. Dexter EZ-Lube Torflex axles with forward self adjusting electric brakes for reduced maintenance. The HSS frame cases the trailer wiring, protecting it from the elements for long-lasting performance.

Tilt Deck Trailers

Low angle tilt decks eliminate the need for ramps and gently lock in place with a hydraulic dampener. Patented Lode N Lok self-locking mechanism locks the deck into place for efficient loading. Standard side steps at the front of the trailer ensures safer, more convenient access to the deck. Bolt-on expanded metal on the rear of the deck delivers increased traction in wet conditions.

Tilt decks have a 16’ tilting deck and 6’ stationary deck for a combined 22’ of usable deck space. Two types of decking - Fir decking provides a harder surface capable of standing more abuse and last longer. Pressure treated spruce or pine decking is more readily available and cost effectively replaceable. Bolt-on fender guards are included, preventing any damage that could compromise safe operation. Bolt-on fork carriers on select models allow forks to be stored and transported under the deck.

Haul some of the largest compact equipment with up to 12,700 lbs payload capacity. Self-adjusting forward brakes enhance control and safety while minimizing maintenance costs. Axles include EZ Lube oil bath bearings ensure peace of mind while reducing maintenance. The HSS tube frame ensures torsional stiffness when traveling with a load, extending trailer life.

Dump Trailers

The performance model has a 2-way spreader gate with barn door plus slide out ramps. Maximize hauling equipment and material with a heavy-duty, spring-assisted, width-adjustable integrated gate and ramps on premium models for the safest gate to load and unload equipment. Brandt gates are constructed with HD keeper pins and latch systems, ensuring a reliable hookup.

The 14' and 16' box lengths optimally fit and balance the weight of compact construction equipment. Available in two box styles with Max Body™ models offering more capacity and optimized for equipment. The box is built with durable 10-gauge steel with only one center seam, reducing debris buildup.

The HSS tube frame ensures torsional stiffness when traveling with a load, extending trailer life. Forward self-adjusting brakes enhance control and safety while minimizing maintenance costs. Available torsion axles or spring suspension in tandem and tri-axle models deliver a smooth ride. Bolt-on key components (including fenders, jacklegs, and couplers) are easy to replace and minimize downtime through more efficient maintenance.

Deckover Flatbed Trailers

Three ramps’ configurations are available with low 16° angle ensures effortless equipment loading. The full-width ramps options provides the flexibility to load all type of equipment delivering the safest platform for machine operators, the equipment, and the trailer itself. 15 and 16-ton platinum models offer hydraulic full-width beavertail ramps for maximum efficiency. Longer freight cargo can be carried and supported on top of deck extending ramps.

Optimized to fit and properly balance the weight of construction equipment and machineries. Premium fir decking provides a hard surface capable of standing more abuse and last longer. Spruce or pine decking is more readily available and cost effectively replaceable. Rub rails and stack pockets on both sides provides multiple tie-down points 5,000 lbs WLL rated. Frame support knee braces on dual wheel trailers deliver increased load support.

These trailers feature 12 to 14 inch I-beams main frame for reduced frame deflection under load. Premium speed rated tires handle the heat dissipation required when operating on highways. Heavy-duty load equalizing spring suspensions share the load between axles. A range of axles and wheel configurations are available for efficient load management.
