Work Ready™ Brandt trailers are purpose-built with durable components and the toughest powder-coat paint finish in the industry to maximize service life. And, they are covered by best-in-class warranty and a nationwide dealer network to ensure your trailer is always ready to work when you are.

Efficient Loading and Unloading.

  • Three ramps’ configurations are available with low 16° angle ensures effortless equipment loading.
  • The full-width ramps options provides the flexibility to load all type of equipment delivering the safest platform for machine operators, the equipment, and the trailer itself.
  • 15 and 16-ton platinum models offer hydraulic full-width beavertail ramps for maximum efficiency.
  • Longer freight cargo can be carried and supported on top of deck extending ramps.

Durable Deck Capabilities

  • Optimized to fit and properly balance the weight of construction equipment and machineries.
  • Premium fir decking provides a hard surface capable of standing more abuse and last longer. Spruce or pine decking is more readily available and cost effectively replaceable.
  • Rub rails and stack pockets on both sides provides multiple tie-down points 5,000 lbs WLL rated.
  • Frame support knee braces on dual wheel trailers deliver increased load support.
Easier To Operate

Efficient loading and unloading with convenient load management features that keep you productive at the worksite.

Increased Safety

Safety is critical on the jobsite, so these models are designed with key features to maximize operator protection.

Easier To Own

Purpose-built design and reliable components ensure a lifespan that matches your equipment.

Premium Structure Design

  • These trailers feature 12 to 14 inch I-beams main frame for reduced frame deflection under load.
  • Premium speed rated tires handle the heat dissipation required when operating on highways.
  • Heavy-duty load equalizing spring suspensions share the load between axles.
  • A range of axles and wheel configurations are available for efficient load management.

Features

Reliable Running Gear

EZ Lube hubs allow easy access to grease points, ensuring efficient lubrication maintenance on the 7K tri-axle configuration and an oil bath on tandem duals.

LED Lighting

Tier 1 LED lights with polycarbonate lenses, sealed connectors, and epoxy-encapsulated electronics deliver significantly more protection than any other trailers in the industry.

Premium Wiring

Braided loom wiring harness with sealed weatherpack connections meet the same rigorous standards of wires found on heavy-duty construction equipment, ensuring life-long protection.

Heavy-Duty Jacklegs

Standard dual-speed jacklegs have 10,000 lbs of lifting capacity while our platinum 16-ton model has the option of a single hydraulically powered jackleg for added versatility.

Bolt-Together Design

All major components such as axles, fenders, jacks, couplers, ramps, and ramp plates (on full-width option) are bolted together, minimizing downtime when maintenance is required.

Versatile Hitch Styles

11-ton models are available with bumper-pull pintle hitches or gooseneck configurations. 15-ton models have standard gooseneck hitches. And, 16-ton models include pintle hitches.

Premium Paint Protection

Premium polyurethane primer and top coat is abrasion-resistant, ensuring these trailers look better for longer while maximizing resale value.

Multiple Configurations
